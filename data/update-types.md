# Update type taxonomy

The routine must classify every update into exactly one of the following six categories. If multiple apply, pick the most material one.

- **Fundraise**: a priced round, SAFE, bridge, debt facility, or term sheet (announced or rumored).
- **Risk**: a material risk signal (legal, regulatory, churn, runway, key-person, security incident, exec departure under bad circumstances).
- **Metrics**: a revenue, growth, retention, usage, or unit-economics data point.
- **Press**: meaningful coverage in tier-1 media, podcasts, conference talks, or notable social posts.
- **Impact Work**: anything related to Primary's Impact team supporting the portco, including recruiting (exec search, talent intros), GTM (sales / marketing / customer intros), and Strategic Finance (modeling, fundraise prep, banker intros).
- **General Update**: any other notable update that doesn't fit the categories above (product launches, exec hires that aren't risk signals, new customers, partnerships, rebrands, office moves, etc.).
